## Implementation Guide

### Slide Design Principles:
1. **Keep it simple**: 6x6 rule (6 bullets, 6 words each)
2. **Use visuals**: Charts, diagrams, icons
3. **Color coding**: Consistent colors for concepts
4. **Progressive disclosure**: Build concepts step-by-step
5. **Active learning**: Include questions, polls, activities

### Color Scheme Suggestions:
- **Blue**: Study designs, methods
- **Red**: Bias, problems
- **Green**: Solutions, positive outcomes
- **Yellow**: Key concepts, warnings
- **Gray**: Background, secondary information

### Animation Tips:
- **Appear**: Build bullet points progressively
- **Fade**: Transition between concepts
- **Zoom**: Emphasize important elements
- **Wipe**: Show relationships between ideas

### Font Guidelines:
- **Titles**: 44pt, Bold
- **Bullets**: 32pt, Regular
- **Captions**: 24pt, Regular
- **Footnotes**: 18pt, Regular

### Best Practices:
1. **One concept per slide**
2. **High contrast colors**
3. **Readable fonts**
4. **Consistent formatting**
5. **Minimize text, maximize visuals**
6. **Include slide numbers**
7. **Add sources/references**

These slide templates provide a professional framework for creating engaging epidemiology lectures with clear visual hierarchy and effective information presentation.
